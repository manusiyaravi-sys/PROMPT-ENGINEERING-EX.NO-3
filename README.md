RESULT
The experiment was conducted to study different prompt engineering techniques including Straightforward Prompting, Tabular Format Prompting, Missing Word Prompting, and Preceding Question Prompting using multiple AI platforms such as ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot.
Straightforward prompting produced clear and direct responses because the instructions were simple and easy for the AI to understand. Tabular format prompting helped present information in a structured way, making comparisons easier and improving readability.
Missing word prompting was effective for testing factual knowledge because the AI could quickly identify the correct term based on context. Preceding question prompting helped guide the AI step by step, improving the relevance of the final answer.
Among the platforms tested, ChatGPT provided highly coherent and detailed responses, while Gemini generated very simple and easy-to-understand explanations. Copilot performed well in providing concise answers.
Overall, the results show that choosing the right prompting technique improves the clarity, accuracy, and usability of AI-generated responses. Different prompting methods are suitable for different types of tasks such as explanation, comparison, knowledge testing, and guided reasoning.
